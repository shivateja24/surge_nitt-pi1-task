About us

What is Surge_NITT?

Surge is a sociology and civic club of NIT Trichy containing young and enthusiastic students with a common inclination towards social interaction,human behaviour,all other sort of things.

Purpose of Surge_NITT

The main purpose of our club is to empower our fellow students in the campus and Incentivize them to Learn,Like and Lead.

Our Goal

Our goal is to follow our passion and also keep a check on our fellow students through frequent surveys,events. We do it with the support of our college and the knowledge that we continuously share among ourselves as a team.
